Example Project

Turning repetitive emails into
ready-to-review drafts.

A business owner was spending multiple hours per day answering similar customer emails. Zuri designed an AI email assistant that reads incoming messages, identifies which ones need a response, drafts replies, and leaves final approval to the human.

  • No automatic sending — human approval stays in control
  • Sensitive messages are flagged instead of answered
  • Repetitive replies become ready-to-review drafts
  • Daily summary delivered to the owner
  • Implemented without replacing the existing email workflow

Less typing. Faster replies. More control.
